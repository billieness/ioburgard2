Ugine Art and Community Center, 2020–21

Curators: Mathilde Sauzet Mattéï, Norbert Duffort

Partners: ESAAA and IAC in Villeurbanne

“The river overflows its banks, and several tributaries feed into it, just like a conversation: leaning against the bar, we talk about collapsology and politics, remaking the world as we wait to see it emerge. Or how, in the current context, to try to move beyond ‘No Future’…”

Norbert Duffort

Featuring works from the IAC Villeurbanne collection by Saadine Afif, Katinka Bock, Jenny Holzer, Guillaume Leblon, Richard Prince, Pipilotti Rist, Ettore Sottsass, and Franz Erhard Walter.
